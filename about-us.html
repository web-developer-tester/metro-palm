About Us

Over the last two decades, we at The Metro Group have created more than 80 iconic real estate projects across the twin cities of Bhubaneswar & Cuttack. We are a real estate development firm rooted within an immense technical ability and experience in the field of construction. We strongly believe that even though our business deals in the realm of tangible products; our success is reliant on the pivotal aspect of being a service-oriented firm. This belief structure has earned us the respect and the pleasurable status of being a household name with odiyas living all over the world. We believe in rendering optimal services across the entire process of creating a real estate product. Starting from the important stage of design and concluding at the finishing stages of construction; our main goal is to achieve a space that enables and complements a client's needs. Our experience across multiple verticals of real estate, such as residential, retail, commercial and industrial has enabled us to maintain a professional mindset across the company that is geared to efficiency. We have expanded into the much-required arena of Contracting & Liaisoning. We have partnered with a host of Corporates, Institutions and Industries that have chosen Odisha as their new destination of operations. Our experience in construction and our local rapport have made us an ideal partner for such entities. We have also provided construction and engineering services to industries of repute like RITES, Sterlite Industries, the Govt. of Karnataka, Indian Oil Corporation Limited, and the Servants of People Society to name a few.
